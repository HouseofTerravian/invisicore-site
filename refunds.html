Refund Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Infrastructure Installations

Due to the nature of infrastructure deployment, all installation fees ($50,000 System Installation and Verticalized Packages) are final and non-refundable unless otherwise stated in writing in the executed service agreement.

SaaS Subscriptions

Monthly subscription plans (Builder, Growth, Pro, Enterprise) are billed in advance. No refunds are provided for partial months. You may cancel your subscription at any time, and access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

Usage-Based Charges

Usage-based charges (API overages, verification checks, publishing posts, webhook events) are billed monthly in arrears and are non-refundable as they reflect services already rendered.

Digital Products

Digital products (including the InvisiCore™ Founder Blueprint) are non-refundable once delivered due to the nature of digital goods.

Exceptions

Refund exceptions may be considered on a case-by-case basis at our sole discretion. To request a review, contact billing@invisicore.com within 7 days of the charge.

Contact

Billing questions may be directed to: billing@invisicore.com
