ADA Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Our Commitment

InvisiCore™ is committed to providing accessible digital experiences for all users, including those with disabilities. We continuously work to improve usability and accessibility across all devices and assistive technologies.

Standards

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines help ensure that digital content is accessible to a wider range of people, including those with visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive disabilities.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios across all interface elements
  • Keyboard-navigable interactive elements
  • Descriptive labels for form fields and controls
  • Responsive design that works across devices and screen sizes
  • Alt text for meaningful images

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our digital properties and make improvements as standards evolve and new best practices emerge.

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at accessibility@invisicore.com. We take all feedback seriously and will work to address concerns promptly.
