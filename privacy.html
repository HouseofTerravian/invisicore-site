Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data required for operation:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name
  • Usage Data: API call volumes, feature usage, login timestamps
  • Payment Data: Processed securely through Stripe; we do not store card numbers
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information for security and performance

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Services
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To communicate service updates and changes
  • To monitor usage for billing and rate limiting
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Stripe (payments), Supabase (database hosting), Vercel (hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

All systems are secured and encrypted. API keys are SHA-256 hashed. Webhook payloads are signed with HMAC-SHA256. Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain account data for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days. Usage logs are retained for 90 days. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@invisicore.com.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the Services.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed to: privacy@invisicore.com
